Sights of Alexandroupolis

Lighthouse

The Lighthouse is one of the most prominent sights of Alexandroupolis. It was constructed in 1850 by the French lighthouse company of the Mediterranean, pursuant to an agreement with the then Turkish government. In fact, it is the largest in all of Europe!

Thrace Ethnological Museum

The Thrace Ethnological Museum was established to preserve historical memory in the broader geographic area of Thrace. It is housed in a rented neoclassical stone building from 1899.

Metropolitan Church Museum

The Metropolitan Church Museum of Alexandroupolis is located opposite our apartment. It was founded in 1976 by the Metropolitan of Alexandroupolis and now Thessaloniki, Anthimos.

Port of Alexandroupolis

The port of Alexandroupolis is one of the most important ports in all of Greece. It is the only major port in Thrace and is the region's most significant commercial hub.
